WHAT WE DO

Ziphire is a web based medium between employers and students looking for part time work. Students can create a simple profile which includes a profile picture, past working experience, current education and a bit about themselves. Employers on the other hand can then search through profiles on Ziphire using key terms such as: *Manchester* *Bartending*. This would then create a list of suitable students.

WHY THIS WAY?

No longer do students have to walk the highstreet handing out CV’s or fill in application forms. One profile is now viewable to hundreds of local employers. Employers can now easily find suitable candidates to hire within four clicks of the mouse.

WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

Once a student has completed their profile they simply log out and wait to be contacted by a range of employers. Employers can contact students directly through their contact details left on their profile page. No dialogue is through Ziphire; meaning recruitment remains 100% in-house
